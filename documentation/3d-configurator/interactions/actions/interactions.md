# Interactions

The **Interaction** action allows for managing other interactions within a project, enabling dynamic control over their execution.

<figure><img src="../../../../.gitbook/assets/image (429).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Settings**

* **Control:**
  * **Trigger** — Forces the execution of another interaction, bypassing its triggers and conditions.
  * **Toggle** — Switches the enable/disable state of another interaction.
  * **Enable** — Makes another interaction available, allowing it to be triggered.
  * **Disable** — Prevents another interaction from being triggered.
* **Interact.** — Select the interaction to be controlled.
